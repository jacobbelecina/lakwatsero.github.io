7 PLACES TO STAY IN Cebu




Cebu : Accommodation


March 30, 2021

David Leh


Explore Cebu and its many tourist attractions from islands and beaches, diving spots, historical landmarks, to local delicacies. Here are some essential travel tips to take note of if you're traveling to this top destination in the Philippines.






When it comes to tourist spots in the Philippines, nothing is as diverse as Cebu, the Queen City of the South, and the oldest city in the country. Outside of Luzon, it's one of the highly urbanized cities in the country next to the capital Manila.

Once the Travel bug bites there is no known antidote, and I know that I shall be happily infected until the end of my life.


  1. Dreamlike Arterra Hotel-Apartment Cebu Seaside 14 Floor

    2. Featuring a restaurant, a fitness center, and a bar, Dreamlike Arterra Hotel-Apartment Cebu Seaside 14 Floor provides accommodations in Mactan with free WiFi and lake views.



  2. Crimson Resort & Spa

    3. A posh hideaway in the island of Mactan with a private beach, an infinity pool, and a spa facility, Crimson Resort & Spa - Mactan Island is a 5-star resort located 11 mi from SM City Cebu and 12 mi from Ayala Mall in Cebu. WiFi is also available throughout the resort.



  3. Shangri-La's Mactan Resort & Spa

    4. Shangri-La's Mactan Resort offers a luxurious beach escape with an outdoor pool and both free in-room WiFi and wired internet. In addition to 9 dining options, it also houses CHI, one of the largest spas in Asia.



  4. Mövenpick Hotel Mactan Island Cebu

    5. Home to the popular Ibiza Beach Club in Mactan, Mövenpick Hotel Mactan Island Cebu is located 0.7 mi from Lapu-Lapu Shrine and 10 mi from SM City Cebu, this 5-star resort features of an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness center, and a private beach area.



  5. Costabella Tropical Beach Hotel

  6. Costabella Tropical Beach Hotel has comfortable accommodations by the white sands of Mactan Island. The hotel features a private beach, 2 outdoor pools and massage treatments. Free private parking is also available.



  7. Plantation Bay Resort and Spa

    8. Featuring one of the world’s largest privately-owned lagoons, Plantation Bay Resort and Spa offers colonial-style rooms on landscaped grounds. It offers 5-star amenities, free scheduled round-trip airport transportation and plenty of activities.



  8. Dusit Thani Mactan Cebu Resort

    9. Located in Mactan, 8.1 mi from Mactan Island Convention Center, Dusit Thani Mactan Cebu Resort provides accommodations with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness center. With a bar, the 5-star resort has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. The accommodations offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.
