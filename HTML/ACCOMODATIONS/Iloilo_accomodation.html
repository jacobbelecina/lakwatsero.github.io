7 PLACES TO STAY IN ILOILO




Iloilo : Accommodation


March 26, 2021

David Leh


Explore the heritage attractions, unique cuisine, and islands of Iloilo. Read this guide to know about the best time to go, where to stay, what to eat, places to visit, and how to plan your itinerary.






Aside from the many eye-popping attractions dotting the province, a rich trail of culture and history, in addition to a list of savory local cuisines, also awaits visitors exploring Iloilo. Make the most of your trip to Iloilo with this travel guide that can help you plan the ultimate holiday.

At the end of the day your feet should be dirty, your hair messy and your eyes sparkling.


  1. Urban Sands Iloilo Hotel

    2. All guest accommodations feature thoughtful amenities to ensure an unparalleled sense of comfort. Throughout the day you can enjoy the relaxing atmosphere of the outdoor pool, spa, massage, pool (kids), garden. With an ideal location and facilities to match, Urban Sands Iloilo Hotel hits the spot in many ways.



  2. Bearland Paradise Resort

    3. Experiencing Bearland Paradise Resort means experiencing the perfect culmination of relaxation and rejuvenation. The surreal essence of peace and solitude will take guests' breath away. The expansive views of blue waters, mountains, and the surrounding nature make this property a truly unique destination for any traveler. The resort offers the convenience of modern amenities combined with the natural charm of a beachfront setting. Whether traveling alone, on a romantic getaway or planning a group outing, Bearland Paradise Resort meets the needs of any traveler.



  3. Adhara Boutique Resort

    4. Ideal for fun and relaxation, Anhawan Beach Resort and Spa is located in the Villa Arevalo area of Iloilo. From here, guests can enjoy easy access to all that the lively city has to offer. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations.



  4. Sheridan Boutique Resort

    5. At Sheridan Boutique Resort, every effort is made to make guests feel comfortable. To do so, the hotel provides the best in services and amenities. To name a few of the hotel's facilities, there are 24-hour room service, daily housekeeping, express check-in/check-out, luggage storage, car park.



  5. Mo2 Westown Hotel and Resort

  6. The hotel also offers many facilities to enrich your stay. While lodging at this wonderful property, guests can enjoy a family room, facilities for disabled guests, business center, nightclub, and safety deposit boxes.



  7. Solina Beach and Nature Resort

    8. Solina Beach and Nature Resort offers impeccable service and all the essential amenities to invigorate the weary traveler. Top features of the property include free Wi-Fi in all rooms, daily housekeeping, wheelchair accessible, 24-hour front desk, and facilities for disabled guests.



  8. Fleur de Liz Inn

    9. Fleur de Liz Inn offers impeccable service and all the essential amenities to invigorate travelers. Guests of the hotel can enjoy on-site features like 24-hour room service, Wi-Fi in public areas, car park, family room, smoking area.
